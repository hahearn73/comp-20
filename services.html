Services
At Rebootable RAM Solutions©, we believe it is our duty to not only provide products to our customers, but also to educate those using our products, as well as offer 24/7 phone support and on-call technicians.
Our products are bought directly from factories and our retrofitted with microcontrollers that can be controlled remotely or from your current workstation. These microcontrollers save snapshots of your memory usage as your computation takes place all in the background at less than a 1% cost in performance. This allows you peace of mind and the ability to reboot and start your processes within seconds of them going down.

What we offer:
  • Complete RAM installation for new and old system hardware
  • Retrofitting existing RAM with our snapshot storing microchips
  • Installation and setup of custom - and FREE - software to monitor any and all of our products
  • Training for your technicians and system administrators in how to use our products and software
  • On-call technicians and 24/7 tech-support by phone
