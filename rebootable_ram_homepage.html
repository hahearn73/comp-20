Why Rebootable RAM?
Random Access Memory (RAM) is what holds the data in your programs while your computer is on. However, all of that data is lost the second power stops being passed through. This can lead to huge losses of data as the result of any failures in the electric grid or simply a short circuit from within your own system. For everyday Users, this is not a problem. But for your large scale calculations, any loss of data can mean hours of lost compute time, and late decisions for your company or R&D team. Rebootable RAM is what you need.

The Solution?
Rebootable RAM Solutions© offers top of the line RAM in all forms that comes prepackaged with configurable microchip controllers which automatically saves RAM state in the case of power outages or loss of battery in mobile devices. Find out more in our services page
